Who we are

Cafe Kittens is a re-homing and care facility with the mission to find a forever home for homeless cats while providing a therapeutic haven for our guests. This unique blend enhances the experience for both cats and guests to 'paws' and take a breather in a seemingly busy world.

Cafe rules

Be gentle and respectful to our cats in every interaction.

Please do not chase, force hold or pick them up.

Only use the specific toys laid out to play with the cats.

Do not feed the cats.

We have a "no shoe" policy in the lounges.

Please keep the sound level low.
